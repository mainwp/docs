## The MainWP Affiliate Program

The MainWP Affiliate program is free and easy to join; it’s quick to sign-up and requires no technical knowledge. Affiliate programs are common throughout the Internet and offer website owners an additional way to profit from their websites. Affiliates generate traffic and sales for commercial websites and, in return, receive a commission payment. You can sign up for the affiliate program in the [MainWP affiliates area](https://mainwp.com/mainwp-affiliates/).

## Benefits

-   Earn a 15% commission for each sale
-   Receive payments via PayPal
-   30-day cookie
-   12-Mos. Recurring commissions on monthly & yearly subscriptions considering a customer retains for a period of 12 months.

[MainWP Program Highlights](https://mainwp.com/mainwp-affiliates/)

## Have Questions? Of Course, You Do. Here’s A Quick Q & A.

### How do I get paid if your plugin is free?

A free customer does not pay any commission; however, if that customer gets the [MainWP Pro](https://mainwp.com/signup/) during your 30-day cookie, you will be paid a commission.

### How do I get paid?

We currently only send payments via Paypal.

### When do I get paid?

We send out payments the first week of the month, 30 days after you receive an affiliate commission.

### Are websites outside of the U.S. eligible for your Affiliate Program?

Yes, we welcome applications to our affiliate program from most countries. Note that you need a Paypal account for the payment.

### Does it cost me anything to become an Affiliate?

It’s free to join our Affiliate Program. There’s no charge to apply.

### Can I promote MainWP on more than one website?

Yes!

### What can I or can’t I do?

Please be sure to read the full Terms and Conditions for being an affiliate on the [affiliate sign up page](https://mainwp.com/mainwp-affiliates/).

### What if I have more questions?

If you have more questions, please [open a ticket](https://mainwp.com/my-account/get-support/).
