Sucuri provides web-based malware scanning of your websites using the latest fingerprinting technology, allowing you to determine if your web applications are out of date, exploited with malware, or even blacklisted by popular search engines.

**Important Notes:**

Due to Sucuri's API TOS, we are restricted from offering a manual scan only. This means that your scheduled scans and scanning of multiple websites at once are not supported.

A Sucuri.net subscription is not required for this extension to function.

## Previous child site reports and settings

<Steps>
<Step title="Login to your MainWP Dashboard">
</Step>
<Step title="Go to the MainWP > Extensions > Manage Sites page">
</Step>
<Step title="Locate the desired site and click the Reports & Settings button">
</Step>
</Steps>


At the bottom of the page, you will find the Sucuri Scanner section.

![Screenshot of Sucuri Scanner section showing SSL verification, previous reports, and scan reminder options](/images/add-ons/sucuri-api-settings.png)

From here, you can:

-   Choose whether or not to **Verify SSL Certificate** for this child site during the scans
-   **Show** and **Delete** any of the previous reports
-   Set to be **reminded** if you don't scan this child site (Never, 1 Day, 1 Week, 1 Month).
