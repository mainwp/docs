MainWP add-ons are available in two tiers designed to meet different needs.

## What You'll Learn

- What's included in the free Essentials tier
- MainWP Pro features and payment options
- How to get started with your chosen plan

---

## Essentials (Free)

Everything you need to get started managing your WordPress sites.

**Includes:**
- Unlimited site management
- All free add-ons
- Security and performance updates
- Community support

## MainWP Pro

Access all premium add-ons with flexible payment options: Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime.

**Includes:**
- All 30+ premium add-ons
- Access to all future add-ons
- Priority ticket support
- Everything in Essentials

### Payment Options

MainWP Pro is available with three payment options to fit your budget:
- **Monthly** - Pay as you go, cancel anytime
- **Yearly** - Best value for ongoing management
- **Lifetime** - One-time payment, lifetime access

## Get Started

Visit [mainwp.com/signup](https://mainwp.com/signup) to compare tiers and choose the plan that's right for you.

<Note>
All MainWP Pro subscriptions include free updates and priority support for the subscription period. Individual add-ons are not sold separately.
</Note>
