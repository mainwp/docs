## What You'll Learn

- Available backup integrations for MainWP
- Supported solutions including UpdraftPlus, Solid Backups, and BackWPup
- Centralized backup management across your network

---

Backup add-ons integrate your favorite WordPress backup solutions with MainWP. Manage backups, view backup status, and restore sites across your entire network from one central Dashboard.

## Available Add-ons

    title="WPvivid"

<Note>
Backup add-ons require the corresponding backup plugin to be installed and activated on your child sites.
</Note>
