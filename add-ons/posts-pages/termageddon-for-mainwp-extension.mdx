Termageddon for MainWP is a Free Extension developed by the team Termageddon. Using this Extension, you can **quickly generate and customize privacy policies for all your Child Sites, saving time and effort**. Furthermore, it ensures that your client sites stay up-to-date with any changes or updates to privacy laws and regulations since Termageddon automatically checks for the privacy policies to reflect any new or amended laws.

## How to edit or delete a Policy Page

After you've created a policy page, the Add Page button will become **Edit Page**. Simply click it to **edit** an existing policy page. ![Edit Page button after policy page has been created](/images/add-ons/51a06843f00f.png) To delete policy pages, select the **Delete** action from the ellipsis menu next to a desired policy page, and confirm the deletion. ![Animation showing how to delete a policy page via the ellipsis menu](/images/add-ons/termageddon-delete-policy.gif)

## Link a child site to a Policy Set

After you've created a Policy Set on Termageddon Dashboard, you can link a Child Site to that Policy Set.
The benefit of linking a child site to the Policy Set is that you will get notified in the **Termageddon for MainWP** extension, in the **Status** column, if an action is required (Statuses: _**Update Required, Incomplete, Pending Invite**_).
If there are no actions required, the Status will display **Active**.

![Status column showing Active status for linked sites](/images/add-ons/91c66e13a066.png)

To link a site, simply click the **Link** button, and then select the Policy Set from the dropdown menu, and then click the **Save Settings** button.

![Animation showing how to link a site to a Policy Set](/images/add-ons/termageddon-link-policy-set.gif)

## Filter Sites

The "Filter" option allows you to filter your Child Sites based on the below statuses:

-   Active
-   Update Required
-   Incomplete
-   Pending Invite

**NOTE:** A child site needs to be linked to a Policy Set, as explained in the chapter above, in order to have **Status** info. ![Filter dropdown showing Active, Update Required, Incomplete, and Pending Invite options](/images/add-ons/584cfc6e6abb.png)
