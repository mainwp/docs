The WP Compress integration for MainWP allows you to manage image optimization and performance improvements across your child sites from your MainWP Dashboard.

## About WP Compress

[WP Compress](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-compress-image-optimizer/) is an image optimization service that automatically compresses and optimizes images to improve website performance and loading times.

## Key Features

- Automatic image compression and optimization
- Lazy loading for images
- WebP and AVIF conversion
- CDN delivery for optimized images
- Bulk optimization across multiple sites
- Performance analytics and reporting

## Requirements

- WP Compress plugin installed on child sites
- WP Compress account (free tier available)
- MainWP Dashboard with Child plugin connected

## Installation

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a WP Compress account">
Sign up for a free account at [WP Compress](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-compress-image-optimizer/).
</Step>
<Step title="Install WP Compress on child sites">
Install and activate the WP Compress plugin on your child sites.
</Step>
<Step title="Connect your account">
Enter your WP Compress API credentials on each child site.
</Step>
<Step title="Sync your MainWP Dashboard">
Sync your MainWP Dashboard to see optimization status across your network.
</Step>
</Steps>
