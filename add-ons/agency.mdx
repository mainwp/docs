Agency add-ons provide professional-grade tools designed specifically for web agencies managing multiple client sites. Generate branded reports, manage team access, and customize your Dashboard experience.

## What You'll Learn

- Available agency add-ons and their capabilities
- Client reporting with Pro Reports
- Team access management
- White labeling options
