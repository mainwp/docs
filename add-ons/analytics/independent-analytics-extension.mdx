The Independent Analytics integration for MainWP allows you to view privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics data from your child sites directly in your MainWP Dashboard.

## About Independent Analytics

[Independent Analytics](https://wordpress.org/plugins/independent-analytics/) is a privacy-focused WordPress analytics plugin that provides visitor statistics without cookies or external services. It's fully GDPR compliant and stores all data on your own server.

## Key Features

- View visitor statistics across all child sites
- Privacy-focused analytics without cookies
- No external services or data sharing
- GDPR and privacy regulation compliant
- Lightweight and performance-optimized

## Requirements

- Independent Analytics plugin installed on child sites
- MainWP Dashboard with Child plugin connected

## Installation

<Steps>
<Step title="Install Independent Analytics on child sites">
Install and activate the [Independent Analytics plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/independent-analytics/) on your child sites.
</Step>
<Step title="Sync your MainWP Dashboard">
After installation, sync your MainWP Dashboard to detect the plugin.
</Step>
</Steps>
