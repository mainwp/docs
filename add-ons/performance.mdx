- WP Rocket integration
- Database maintenance tools
- Image optimization with WP Compress

---

Performance add-ons help you optimize and maintain your WordPress sites. Manage caching, enable maintenance mode, and integrate with popular performance plugins to keep your sites running fast.

## Available Add-ons

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Cache Control"
    img="/images/icons/addons-sm/cache-control.png"
    href="/add-ons/performance/cache-control-extension"
  >
    Manage cache across multiple sites. Clear, purge, and control caching for popular cache plugins from one location.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="WP Rocket"
    img="/images/icons/addons-sm/rocket.png"
    href="/add-ons/performance/rocket-extension"
  >
    Manage WP Rocket settings across your network. Control caching, preloading, and optimization settings centrally.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Maintenance"
    img="/images/icons/addons-sm/maintenance.png"
    href="/add-ons/performance/maintenance-extension"
  >
    Enable maintenance mode across sites. Display custom maintenance pages while you work on updates or changes.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="WP Compress"
    img="/images/icons/addons-sm/wp-compress.png"
    href="/add-ons/performance/wp-compress-extension"
  >
    Manage WP Compress image optimization. Control compression settings and monitor optimization status network-wide.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
Performance add-ons like WP Rocket require the corresponding plugin to be installed and licensed on your child sites.
</Note>
