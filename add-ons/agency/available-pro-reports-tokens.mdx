  Complete reference of all available tokens for Pro Reports templates including
  client tokens, site tokens, report tokens, and section tokens for customizing
  your professional reports.
addonType: extension

## What You'll Learn

- Client, site, and report tokens for basic information
- Section tokens for looping through activity data
- Counter tokens for displaying totals
- Extension-specific tokens for analytics and security

---

<Info>
**Extension Add-on** - This add-on provides standalone functionality within MainWP Dashboard. No third-party plugins required.
</Info>
