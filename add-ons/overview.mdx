## Getting Started with Add-ons

1. Navigate to **MainWP > Extensions** in your Dashboard
2. Click **Install Extensions** to browse available add-ons
3. Select the add-ons you want to install
4. Click **Install** and then **Activate Extensions**

<Note>
Some add-ons require third-party plugins to be installed on your child sites. Check individual add-on documentation for specific requirements.
</Note>
