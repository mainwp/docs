## What You'll Learn

- Available analytics integrations for MainWP
- Supported platforms including Google Analytics, Fathom, and Matomo
- Privacy-focused and self-hosted analytics options

---

Analytics add-ons bring your site metrics directly into the MainWP Dashboard. View traffic data, search performance, and visitor insights across all your managed sites without leaving your Dashboard.
