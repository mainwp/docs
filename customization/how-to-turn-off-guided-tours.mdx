MainWP guided tours provide information about features on each Dashboard page. Once you're familiar with the interface, you can disable these tours.

## What You'll Learn

- How to disable guided tours from settings
- How to disable tours from any page

---

## What Are Guided Tours

Guided tours are popup overlays that highlight and explain features as you navigate through the Dashboard.

![MainWP guided tour popup showing feature information](/images/faq/guided-tours-toggle.png)

---

## Method 1: From Settings

<Steps>
<Step title="Open MainWP Tools">
Navigate to **MainWP > Settings > MainWP Tools**.
</Step>
<Step title="Find the tours setting">
Locate the **Enable MainWP guided tours** toggle.
</Step>
<Step title="Disable tours">
Toggle the setting to **Off**.
</Step>
<Step title="Save changes">
Click **Save Settings**. Guided tours are now disabled.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Method 2: From Any Page

You can toggle tours off directly from the interface:

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Need Help">
Click the **Need help** icon (question mark) in the top right corner of any MainWP page.
</Step>
<Step title="Toggle tours off">
Find **Switch to enable or disable tours** and toggle it off.

![Animation showing how to toggle off guided tours from the Need help menu](/images/faq/guided-tours-animation.gif)
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Re-enable Guided Tours

To turn tours back on:

1. Go to **MainWP > Settings > MainWP Tools**
2. Toggle **Enable MainWP guided tours** to On
3. Click **Save Settings**

Or use the **Need help** menu to toggle tours back on.
