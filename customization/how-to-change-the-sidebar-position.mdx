  Change the MainWP Dashboard sidebar position from left to right using the
  Sidebar Options section. The page reloads automatically to apply changes.
---

You can position the MainWP Dashboard sidebar on either the left or right side of the screen. The change takes effect immediately.

## What You'll Learn

- How to access sidebar position settings
- Available position options

---

## Change Sidebar Position

<Steps>
<Step title="Find Sidebar Options">
Locate the **Sidebar Options** section at the top of the sidebar.
</Step>
<Step title="Expand the options">
Click to expand the Sidebar Options dropdown.

![Sidebar Options dropdown showing position selection](/images/faq/sidebar-position-option.png)
</Step>
<Step title="Select your preferred position">
Choose either **Left** or **Right** position. The page reloads automatically and the sidebar moves to the selected position.
</Step>
</Steps>
