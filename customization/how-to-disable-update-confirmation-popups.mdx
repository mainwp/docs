MainWP displays confirmation dialogs before running updates on child sites. You can disable these popups if you prefer to run updates without the extra confirmation step.

## What You'll Learn

- How to disable update confirmation popups
- Available confirmation options

---

## Disable Update Confirmations

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Settings">
Navigate to **MainWP > Settings**.
</Step>
<Step title="Find the option">
Locate the **Update Confirmations** setting.
</Step>
<Step title="Select your preference">
Choose your preferred behavior:

| Option | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| Enabled | Show confirmation before every update |
| Disabled | Run updates immediately without confirmation |
</Step>
<Step title="Save settings">
Scroll to the bottom and click **Save Settings**.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## When to Disable Confirmations

Consider disabling confirmations if:
- You regularly update sites in bulk
- You're confident in your update process
- You use staging or backups to test updates first

Keep confirmations enabled if:
- You want an extra safety check before updates
- Multiple team members manage the Dashboard
- You prefer reviewing each update before it runs
