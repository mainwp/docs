You can hide widgets you don't use from the Overview page. This reduces clutter and helps you focus on the information that matters to your workflow.

## What You'll Learn

- How to hide widgets from the Overview page
- Where to find the widget visibility settings

---

## Hide Widgets

<Steps>
<Step title="Open MainWP Tools">
Navigate to **MainWP > Settings > MainWP Tools**.
</Step>
<Step title="Find widget visibility option">
Locate the **Hide unwanted widgets** section.
</Step>
<Step title="Select widgets to hide">
Check the boxes next to each widget you want to hide from the Overview page.
</Step>
<Step title="Save your changes">
Click **Save Settings**. The selected widgets no longer appear on the Overview page.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Restore Hidden Widgets

To show a hidden widget again:

1. Return to **MainWP > Settings > MainWP Tools**
2. Uncheck the widget in the **Hide unwanted widgets** section
3. Click **Save Settings**
